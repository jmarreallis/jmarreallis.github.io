Provenance of the Bust by JMarr

Of Mr. Edward P. Allis
By Sculptor and Engraver John Marr

The following section is derived from the 1909 Columbia University Catalogue of Sculpture1

This bust is a life size bronze portrait of the late Edward P. Allis.

It is directed and facing front with the signature "J. Marr," short for the sculptor John Marr, on the back of the bust.

Originally this bust held the inscription "Edward P. Allis" on base however it has since been removed.

This bust was donated to the Mechanical Engineering department, specifically to be placed in the Engineering Experimental Laboratory, by E. P. Allis & Co., Milwaukee, Wis., March 1, 1901.


1 The Frick Collection, Catalogue of Sculpture, Columbia University Quarterly, September 1909
